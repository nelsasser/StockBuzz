StockBuzz

About

StockBuzz was created by Nick Elsasser, Sam Pasquesi, Tiffany Duong, and Robert Hammond on July 21, 2018 for the 2018 Chicago Hackathon.

Its purpose is to give a quick, organized overview for prospective investors of a company stock.
What better way to predict a stock price than understand the demand for a service or product? Look to the people!
That's why StockBuzz uses public twitter posts and gives you an interactive view of the public opinion.
